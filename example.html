Cheesecake Recipe with Raspberry Sauce

Here is an easy and quick recipe

Ingredients

  • 10 Honey Graham crackers
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 4 packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 pints raspberries

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
  3. Beat all ingredients except crackers, reserved two tablespoons sugar and raspberries until smooth
  4. Chill the cheesecake overnight
  5. Simmer raspberries and reserved sugar until rasberries are soft.
  6. Layer crackers, cake and raspberries
